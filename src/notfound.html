Aviation

My biggest passion outside of school is aviation. I have been determined to become a pilot as long as I can remember. Currently, I achieve this passion by spending my time on Vatsim.

VATSIM (short for the Virtual Air Traffic Simulation Network) is a completely free online platform which allows virtual pilots, wherever they are in the world, to connect their flight simulators into one shared virtual world. VATSIM also simulates air traffic control in this virtual world, creating the ultimate as-real-as-it-gets experience for you, the virtual aviation enthusiast. Source

I currently hold my PPL rating on Vatsim with just over 350 flying hours logged. I also have recently achieved my S1 Controller rating from Boston Virtual ARTCC.
